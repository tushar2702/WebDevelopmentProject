This weekend, I worked on LINQ and studied the basic concepts of how LINQ works and how basic queries are written in LINQ. The more important thing that I learnt is how to use Linq-to-sql classes and make connection with the database as well as use these classes with auto generated code. If you want to learn a step by step process of how to get connected with LINQ and basic things on linq, it is best to refer to Prof. Rasala's LINQ notes on his site. It's beautiful and systematic. Follow each and every step and you won't fail.

For my experiment that demonstrates the use of LINQ, I used Northwind database and basic queries of selecting particular columns from specific tables of the Northwind database. Fairly simple experiment but good to start with LINQ.
