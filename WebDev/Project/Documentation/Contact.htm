Contact Us


On the Contact Us page, I have provided basic contact information. I have also shown how to reach us on Google Maps by embedding the Google Map with the marker set at the place where we are located. I have used Northeastern University address as a dummy address for my hotel.

I also have two other pages associated with it. One is sending feedback. The user can send me feedback about my hotel by entering information such as his or her name, email address, subject and comments about my hotel. Here I have used System.net.mail namespace and the email will be sent to my gmail address that I created for this website xyz1abc23@gmail.com.

One more thing that I have shown here is a google widget which gives the user directions from the location he enters to the hotel.

Important Technologies used in this page:

  • Google Map API
  • Google Map widget for showing directions
  • C# System.net.mail namespace for feedback
